About Lucky Shrub

Lucky Shrub, based in Tucson, Arizona, is a medium-sized garden design company specializing in garden design, creation, and landscaping. Our team, led by Jason and Maria, shares a passion for plants and transforming spaces into proud oases.

Jason, our lead garden architect, oversees all designs and leads our landscaping team. Maria manages the company's marketing efforts and supervises our nursery, offering both indoor and outdoor plants.
